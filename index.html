Selective Attention
It is the ability to concentrate on a task over a period of time without being distracted. At any given moment, we are all subjected to numerous sensory information
But in most cases, we don’t have to pay attention to each and every one of them. And this is where Selective Attention is used, to focus on what is important and tune out other background information.
